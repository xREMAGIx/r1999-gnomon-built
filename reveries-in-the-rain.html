Depths of Myth

In [Reveries in the Rain], the [Depths of Myth] is the most complete and expansive part of the consciousness remnants and serves as our primary exploration target. Each small section within the remnants is a stage full of the unknown and challenges.
[Depths of Myth] is divided into multiple Zones, each with 10 stages that must be cleared sequentially.
The 10th stage in each Zone is a special one, containing several sub-stages.
Clearing each stage yields Euphoria materials; clearing each 10th special stage yields Clear Drops besides the Euphoria materials.

Trance

Clearing the 5th stage of the [Euphotic Zone] unlocks the [Trance] feature, allowing you to raid Euphoria materials. Clearing deeper in the [Depths of Myth] increases material rewards from [Trance].
4 [Trance] chances are restored at 5:00 AM every Monday, with a maximum storage of 8 [Trance] chances

Isle of Echoes

Clearing the 5th stage of [Euphotic Zone] in [Depths of Myth] unlocks the Myth Manifest trial mode, [Isle of Echoes], where you can unlock and enhance [Myth Manifests] to gain powerful abilities.
In [Isle of Echoes], each stage requires 4 characters and the [Myth Manifest] specified for the challenge.
Clearing first layer of any stage in [Isle of Echoes] unlocks the corresponding [Myth Manifest] to assist your team.
Clearing [Myth Manifest] stages increases its level and grants 1st Clearance rewards. [Myth Manifests] in [Isle of Echoes] are available for limited periods and in turn, allowing players to unlock and experience various [Myth Manifests].

Myth Manifests

As manifestations of consciousness and condensations of arcane powers, the [Myth Manifests] guard the remnants they are bound to. In intense battles, they assist your team with active skills and passive effects.
During battles, when conditions are met, you can use a [Myth Manifest]‘s incantations, which do not consume your team’s AP. The incantation takes effect with your other incantations during your turn of action.
[Myth Manifests] also possess unique passive effects. Once activated, these effects grant significant buffs to your team. As the [Myth Manifests] level up, their passive effects become more potent, providing a strategic advantage that can turn the tide of battle.
[Myth Manifests] can also grow, enhancing their stats and skill effects. Clear corresponding [Myth Manifest] stages in [Isle of Echoes] to level up [Myth Manifests] and gain map fragments to light up the Mind Map.

Vibrations of Volatility

[Voyages of Volatility] consists of 3 stages, each recording independent challenge results.
Characters and Myth Manifests who clear one stage are locked and cannot participate in other stages.
Clear the stages to earn points. If you fail to defeat your enemies within the required rounds or if all allied characters die, the stage is ended and points are calculated.
Each stage has 3 difficulty levels to choose from before starting. Higher difficulty yields more points.
[Voyages of Volatility] stages refresh periodically, featuring 3 [Myth Manifests] for selection.
Achieving the required total points across all 3 stages completes the challenge and unlocks rewards.
