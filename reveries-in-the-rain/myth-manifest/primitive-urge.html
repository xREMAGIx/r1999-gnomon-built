Primitive Urge

Ritual

Information

Primitive Urge will be suitable for character specialize in casting Ultimate.

Manifest skill

Indulging in Dreams

Consume all Energon (requires at least 3 points of Energon) to deploy the array
[Crimson Dreamscape]
for 2 rounds; for each additional point of Energon consumed, extend the array by 1 round; when the array disappears, cast the ultimate
[Into the Abyss]
.

Passive

Passive I
Whenever other allies gain 5 points of Moxie, gain Energon +1. Excess Moxie on allies are temporarily stored (up to 2 points of Moxie can be stored). At the end of the round, the character’s stored Moxie is used to replenish their own Moxie if able (only the largest temporary Moxie storage in play applies; storages don’t stack).
Passive II(unlocks at Lv. 5)
While in the
[Crimson Dreamscape]
array, after another ally casts a damage-type ultimate, cast
[Passionate Embrace]
as a follow-up attack.
Passive III (unlocks at Lv. 8)
After an ally casts an ultimate, recovers (Missing HP x10%) HP for all allies.
Passive IV(unlocks by clearing all First Echo stages)
Stronger Afflatus DMG Factor +20%, Ultimate Might +60%.
When this unit triggers a follow-up attack, the attack gains Penetration Rate +100% and Incantation Might +100%.

Mindmap

* Click on a node to see its content

Alluring Stare

When attacking with [Into the Abyss], if there’s less than 3 enemy units on the field, deal an additional 75% Mental DMG for each unit missing on the field.

Awakening Hours

At the end of the round, if Energon has reached at least 5, the array ends immediately.

Ceaseless Indulgence

During [Crimson Dreamscape], Ultimate Moxie Consumption -1.

Decadence's Toll

[Into the Abyss] inflicts an additional 2 stacks of
[Weakness]
on the target.

Decadent Indulgence

When an allied character is in the [Crimson Dreamscape] array, the character gains Ultimate Might +10% when attacking.

Decadent Indulgence II

During [Crimson Dreamscape], all enemies get an additional ATK -5% (Primitive Urge gains an equal amount of ATK in turn).

Decadent Indulgence III

During [Crimson Dreamscape], allies gain Ultimate DMG Taken -20%.

Enchanting Surge

When allied characters enter battle, Moxie +1.

Enrapturing Embrace

[Passionate Embrace] deals 50% more Mental DMG.

Essence Absorption

When entering battle, initial Energon +1.

Essence Absorption II

During [Crimson Dreamscape], after an ally casts an ultimate, the ally with the lowest Moxie gains Moxie +1.

Essence Absorption III

When an ally loses Moxie, recovers (Max HP x10%) HP.

Exhaustion

When [Crimson Dreamscape] ends, inflict
[Seal]
on all enemies for 1 round.

Into Dream's Embrace

When [Crimson Dreamscape] is deployed, the enemy with the highest Moxie gets Moxie -1.

Into Dream's Embrace II

When [Crimson Dreamscape] is deployed, all allies gain
[Moxie Guard]
for 1 round.

Lingering Dreams

After casting [Into the Abyss], the boss gains Ultimate Might +10% (stackable).

Nostalgic Past

[Into the Abyss] deals -50% Mental DMG; when the array ends, recover Energon +1.

Prolonged Dreams

When deploying the array by consuming at least 4 Energon, the array’s duration is extended by 1 round.

Restlessness

Store an additional 1 point of excess Moxie.

Serene Quietude

During [Crimson Dreamscape], at the end of the round, all allies gain 1 stack of
[Rippling Energy]
.

Surrendered in Temptations

Ultimate Might +20%, Max Energon -2, array cooldown -2, and array duration -1 round; when the array ends, Energon +1.

Thanks for the Hospitality

During [Crimson Dreamscape], each time after an ally casts an ultimate, the next [Into the Abyss] deals an additional 100% Mental DMG, and the duration of [Neg Status] on enemies are extended by 1 round.

Ultimate Might

Allies gain Ultimate Might +6%.

Ultimate Might

Allies gain Ultimate Might +12%.

Unrelenting Whispers

After an ally casts a damage-type ultimate, even if not under an Array, casts a
[Licked by Thorns]
as a follow-up attack (no longer casts [Passionate Embrace]).

Weakness

Neg Status

DMG Bonus -25% when attacking (reduced by 1 stack on trigger).

Seal

Control

Cannot use Ultimate.

Moxie Guard

Pos Status

Gains immunity to Moxie reduction.

Rippling Energy

Pos Status

Ultimate Might +5% (stacks up to 10 times, undispellable).

Licked by Thorns

1-target attack. Deals 200% Mental DMG.

Crimson Dreamscape

[Array] effect: Allied characters gain Ultimate Might +30%. At the start of the enemy’s round, enemies get 1 effect from
[Collection of Debuffs]
for 2 rounds; enemies also get ATK -5%, while Primitive Urge gains an equivalent increase in ATK.

Collection of Debuffs

There are 7 effects:
[Blind]
,
[Nasty Wound]
,
[Mis-aim]
,
[Weakness]
,
[Scared]
,
[Confusion]
and
[Fracture I]
. Prioritizes effects not yet applied.

Blind

Neg Status

DMG Bonus -30% when attacking 1 target.

Nasty Wound

Neg Status

Healing Taken -50%.

Mis-aim

Neg Status

DMG Bonus -30% when attacking multiple targets.

Scared

Neg Status

Increases Mental DMG taken by 25% (-1 stack after triggering).

Confusion

Neg Status

Critical Resist -25% (can stack; each stack is timed independently).

Fracture I

Neg Status

When attacked, takes additional (the user’s ATK x20%) Genesis DMG.

Into the Abyss

Mass attack. Deals 300% Mental DMG to all enemies; consumes all Energon, with each point dealing an additional 100% Mental DMG.

Passionate Embrace

1-target attack. Deals 100% Mental DMG and inflicts Moxie -1 on the target.
