Star of Misfortune

Critical
Eureka
Genesis DMG
Extra Action

Information

Star of Misfortune will be suitable for Star character, with specialize in increasing Critical, Eureka and Genesis DMG.

Manifest skill

Stellar Compression

Can only be cast while not in the Gather Energy status. Enter the Gather Energy status and cast
[Stellar Collapse]
at the end of the next round; entering the Gather Energy status costs no Energon, and casting
[Stellar Collapse]
consumes all Energon and enhances its effects based on the amount of Energon consumed.

Passive

Passive I
When entering battle and at the start of every 2 rounds, Energon +1.
Passive II(unlocks at Lv. 5)
After casting an ultimate, gain 1 stack of
[Discernment]
for self.
Passive III (unlocks at Lv. 8)
This unit’s attacks are always critical hits.
Passive IV(unlocks by clearing all First Echo stages)
Stronger Afflatus DMG Factor +20%. Critical DMG +60%.
When starting to gather energy, immediately gain 1 Energon.

Mindmap

ATK

Star allies gain ATK +5%.

Critical DMG

Star allies gain Critical DMG +10%.

Critical Rate

Star allies gain Critical Rate +8%.

DMG Bonus

Star allies gain DMG Bonus +6%.

Expanded Thinking

Star allies gain Max Eureka +1.

Flash of Eureka I

When a Star ally consumes Eureka, gain 1 stack of
[Lucky]
for self; each ally can gain up to 10 stacks of
[Lucky]
this way.

Flash of Eureka II

For each Eureka a Star ally consumes, the ally has a 10% chance to recover 1 Eureka.

Flash of Eureka III

When a Star ally consumes Eureka, gain 1 [Energon] (triggers up to 1 time per character per round).

Genesis Bonus

Star allies gain Genesis DMG +10%.

Genesis Bound I

[Insistent Beliefs]
deals with an additional (own ATK x25%) Genesis DMG.

Genesis Bound II

The ultimate deals an additional (own ATK x80%) Genesis DMG.

Genesis Bound III

Gain ATK +5% for self; after casting an ultimate, gain Energon +1.

Incantation Might

Star allies gain Incantation Might +6%.

Intro to Astronomy

Gain the passive skill
[Insistent Beliefs]
: Attacks a random enemy, dealing 100% Mental DMG and gaining 1 [Energon]; this effect is casted once every 5 rounds, and cannot be casted while gathering energy.

Mindset Correction

After casting an ultimate, gain 1 stack of
[Discernment]
for self.

Penetration Rate

Star allies gain Penetration Rate +8%.

Practical Studies I

After casting an ultimate, grant 1 stack of
[Schemed]
to all allies.

Practical Studies II

The Ultimate is treated as an extra action, its Gather Energy is reduced by 1 round, but suffers DMG Dealt -50%.

Practical Studies III

After a Star ally performs an extra action, gain 1 [Energon]; at the end of the round, if 2 extra actions were taken in the round, gain 1 additional Energon.

Rapid Ideation

[Insistent Beliefs]
cast frequency is changed to once every 4 rounds.

Reflective Rumination

[Insistent Beliefs]
inflicts an additional stack of
[Confusion]
on a random target.

Sudden Creativity I

At the start of the round, 50% chance to inflict 1 stack of
[Confusion]
on a random target for 1 round.

Sudden Creativity II

After casting an ultimate, grant 2 stacks of
[Discernment]
to all allies.

Sudden Creativity III

When a Star character scores a Critical Hit, gain 1 [Energon] (triggers up to 1 time per character per round).

Ultimate Might

Star allies gain Ultimate Might +6%.

Stellar Collapse

Deals 100% DMG to all enemies; for each [Energon] consumed, DMG +50%; if at least 6 [Energon] was consumed by this action, casts [Stellar Collapse] again with the same power.

Discernment

Pos Status

Critical DMG +40% (-1 stack when scoring a critical hit with an incantation).

Lucky

Pos Status

Critical Rate +5%, Critical DMG +5% (up to 50 stacks, undispellable).

Schemed

Pos Status

When triggering an extra action, DMG Dealt +5% (up to 50 stacks, undispellable).

Confusion

Neg Status

Critical Resist -25% (can stack; each stack is timed independently).

Insistent Beliefs

Attacks a random enemy, dealing 100% Mental DMG and gaining 1 [Energon]; every 5 rounds, if not in the Gather Energy status, this effect is casted automatically.
