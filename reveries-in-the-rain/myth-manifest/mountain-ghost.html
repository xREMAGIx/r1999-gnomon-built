Mountain Ghost

Rank Up
Genesis DMG
Petrify

Information

Mountain Ghost will be suitable for Plant character, with specialize in
[Poison]
,
[Petrify]
and Rank up incantation.

Manifest skill

Thunderous Skies

Costs 100 Energon to cast. Mass attack. Deals 300% Mental DMG to all enemies; gain Incantation Draw Limit +2 on the next round, and the top incantation of the Spellbox gets rank +1.

Passive

Passive I
When entering battle, recover 50 Energon. Right before the incantations in the AP Area resolve, add a rank 3 incantation
[Coming Storm]
to the spell queue, and recover Energon equal to the total ranks of the incantations played, multiplied by 3.
Passive II(unlocks at Lv. 5)
When a unit with [Neg Status] dies, copy 1 random [Neg Status] onto another random enemy.
Passive III (unlocks at Lv. 8)
After casting an ultimate, the 2nd and 3rd incantations from the top of the Spellbox get rank +1.
Passive IV(unlocks by clearing all First Echo stages)
Stronger Afflatus DMG Factor +20%, ATK +30%.
When attacking, deal (own ATK x150%) Genesis DMG.

Mindmap

100% Chance of Rain

When Mountain Ghost casts
[Coming Storm]
, Mental DMG deals +50%.

Acidic Haze

When Mountain Ghost casts
[Coming Storm]
, inflict 1 additional stack of
[Weakness]
.

ATK

Plant allies gain ATK +3%.

Converting Excess

When Mountain Ghost casts an ultimate, the 4th incantation from the top of the Spellbox gets rank +1.

Dense Overgrowth I

When a Plant ally casts a rank 2/3 incantation, Penetration Rate +8%.

Dense Overgrowth II

When a Plant ally casts a rank 2/3 incantation, recover 3 Energon.

Dense Overgrowth III

When a Plant ally casts a rank 2/3 incantation, there’s a 25% chance to cast it 1 additional time.

DMG Bonus

Plant allies gain DMG Bonus +6%.

DMG Taken Reduction

Plant allies gain DMG Taken Reduction +6%.

Dominate Foe

Plant allies gain Stronger Afflatus Damage +6%.

High Voltage Field

When Mountain Ghost casts
[Coming Storm]
, there’s a 50% chance it will target an additional enemy.

Hotbed Cultivation I

Plant allies get status Healing Taken +20% and skill Healing Taken -20%.

Hotbed Cultivation II

When a Plant ally casts an incantation, the ally gains
[Cure]
for a round duration equal to incantation rank.

Induce Crisis

When a unit with a [Neg Status] dies, copy 2 additional random [Neg Status] effects onto another random enemy.

Intense Treatment I

Plant allies get skill Healing Taken +20% and status Healing Taken -20%.

Intense Treatment II

When a Plant ally casts an incantation, recover (incantation rank x ATK x15%) HP for self.

Repel Foe

When a Plant ally is attacked by a non-stronger Afflatus, Final DMG Taken Reduction +6%.

Rising Mass

Starting from rounds 1/2 and at every 4 rounds thereafter, characters in positions #1/#2 gain rank +1 to a random incantation.

Stringent Regulations I

When an enemy with a [Control] status dies, there’s a 50% chance to copy 1 random [Control] status onto another random enemy.

Stringent Regulations II

At the end of the round, for each enemy that did not take an action for the round, recover 7 Energon.

Stringent Regulations III

When Mountain Ghost casts an ultimate, the main target gets
[Petrify]
for 1 round.

Sunny with Showers

After playing incantations, recover additional Energon equal to the total ranks of the incantations in the AP Area.

Toxicology Research I

Mountain Ghost’s inflicted
[Weakness]
is converted to 2 rounds of
[Poison]
.

Toxicology Research II

At the end of the round, for each instance of
[Poison]
on enemies, recover 1 Energon.

Toxicology Research III

When Mountain Ghost attacks, copy 3 instances of
[Poison]
from the main target onto a random enemy.
