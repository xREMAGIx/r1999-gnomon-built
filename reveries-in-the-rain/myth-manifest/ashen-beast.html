Ashen Beast

HP Sacrifice
Extra Action
Buff

Information

Ashen Beast will be suitable for Mineral character, with specialize in increasing DMG of HP sacrifice team and Follow-up Attack.

Manifest skill

Rolling Smog

Mass attack. Consumes 50% Smog Density and deals 150% Reality DMG to all enemies; when casted again in the same round, this skill is enhanced to
[Consuming Sunlight's Hue]
.

Passive

Passive I
Each round, Smog Density +25%.
Passive II(unlocks at Lv. 5)
At the end of the round, recovers (Missing HP x10%) HP for all allies.
Passive III (unlocks at Lv. 8)
After actively casting 4 a total of incantations, dispels [Stats Down] and [Control] effects from all allies.
Passive IV(unlocks by clearing all First Echo stages)
Stronger Afflatus DMG Factor +20%, DMG Bonus +45%.
After taking an action, the subsequent attacks deal an additional 20% Reality DMG; this effect stacks up to 10 times, and casting
[Sunlights Funeral]
grants an additional 1 stack.

Sturdiness

Pos Status

When attacked, DMG Taken Reduction +25% (-1 stack after trigger).

Heat Overload

Special

Cannot act, Cannot gain Smog Density.

Collection of Smog Status

Includes all Stats Up effects, except the ones related to HP and Critical Techniques.

Smoggy Haze

Pos Status

When performing an extra action, Incantation Might +10% and Penetration Rate +10% (stacks up to 5 times).

Gnashing Bite

1-target attack. Deals 100% Mental DMG.

Weakness Exposed

Neg Status

When being attacked by an extra action, DMG Taken +30% (stack up to 1 time; removed after trigger).

Sunlights Funeral

Mass attack. Deals 300% Reality DMG to all enemies and grants 1 stack of
[Rousing Morale]
to all allies; enters the
[Heat Overload]
status for 3 rounds.

Rousing Morale

Pos Status

DMG Bonus +50% when attacking (reduced by 1 stack on trigger).

Fate Amidst the Fog

Special

At the end of the round, if HP is above 50%, lose 15% of current HP and gain a Shield with (Missing HP x200%) HP; if HP is below 50%, gain a Shield with (own ATK x100%) HP; these Shields last 1 round and grant DMG Taken -20%.

Consuming Sunlight's Hue

Mass attack. Consumes 50% Smog Density, deals 150% Reality DMG to all enemies, and grants 1 stack of
[Sturdiness]
