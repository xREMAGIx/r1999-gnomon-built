Harbinger of the New Era

Enhanced Dynamo
Enhanced Action

Information

Harbinger of the New Era will be suitable for characters in Dynamo team or Critical DMG.

Manifest skill

Full Destruction Sequence

Mass attack. Deals 300% Reality DMG. If Natural Energon is more than Primal Energon, adds
[Natural Enhancement]
effect to this attack. If Natural Energon is not more than Primal Energon, adds
[Primal Enhancement]
effect to this attack.
Energon can be gained at the start of the round via the passive skill.

Natural Repellence

Neg Status

Mental DEF -12%, stackable.

Dynamo

Special

A product of technology used for meticulous machinery (up to 30 stacks, undispellable).

Natural Energon

Red energy. Critical Rate +8%, Critical DMG +8%. Gains Ultimate Reality DMG bonus.

Primal Energon

Blue energy. Incantation Might +5%, Ultimate Might +5%. Gains ATK bonus when casting Ultimate.

Primal Affinity

Pos Status

Ultimate Might +8% (stackable).

Electromagnetic Affinity

Pos Status

Ultimate Might +8%, Incantation Might +8% (stackable).

Pulsing Field

At the start of the round, Pulsing Field upgrades based on the
[Dynamo]
transferred into it. At 50+, upgrades into
[Pulsing Field Lv.2]
. At 120+, upgrades into
[Pulsing Field Lv.3]
. When Pulsing Field disappears, all
[Dynamo]
transferred into it are also cleared.

Pulsing Field Lv.2

All allies gain Incantation Might +25% and DMG Bonus +15% for 3 rounds.

Pulsing Field Lv.3

All allies gain Ultimate Might +35%, Incantation Might +25%, and DMG Bonus +15% for 2 rounds.

Natural Enhancement

This attack additionally deals (Energon x100%) Reality DMG and dispels all buffs from all enemies. Inflicts (Energon cost number) stacks of
[Natural Repellence]
on all enemies.

Primal Enhancement

This attack gains ATK +(Energon x20%) and purifies all negative statuses from all allies. Grants (Energon cost number) stacks of
[Primal Affinity]
to all allies.
