Unsparing

Pos Status

DMG Dealt +5% (up to 50 stacks, undispellable).

Sluggish

Neg Status

DMG Bonus -5% (stackable up to 50 times, undispellable).

Guardian's Blessing I

When being attacked, ripostes with Incantation 1 against the enemy.

Bane of All Evil · Star

Pos Status

After being attacked by a non-ultimate attack, gains Reality DEF +5% and Mental DEF +5% for 3 rounds (stackable, undispellable).

Bane of All Evil · Plant

Pos Status

Immune to Poison, Burn, and Petrify (undispellable).

Bane of All Evil · Beast

Pos Status

When attacked by an ultimate, gains Reality DEF +10% and Mental DEF +10% for 3 rounds (stackable, undispellable).

Bless Life

Pos Status

Cannot gain Moxie. When dying, recovers (Max HP x50%) HP.

Stability

Pos Status

AP +1, up to 4 stacks.

Harmony

Pos Status

DMG Taken Reduction +30% (stacks up to 4 times).

Burn

Neg Status

Healing Taken -15% (unstackable). At the end of the round, takes (the holder’s ATK x4%) Genesis DMG (stackable up to 30 times, multiple stacks of Burn is regarded as 1 [Neg Status], removes 50% of the stacks when triggered).

Poison

Neg Status

After a round ends, takes (the caster’s ATK x30%) Genesis DMG. Can stack.

Equity

Pos Status

DMG Bonus +15%. At the start of the round, recovers (Missing HP x5%) HP (stackable up to 4 times).

Arise, Qiangliang!

DMG Taken Reduction +50%. Casts 3 types of incantations that grant
[Stability]
,
[Harmony]
, and
[Equity]
.
