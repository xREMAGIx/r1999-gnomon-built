Stage info

  • The enemies are Necrologist and ghosts.
  • Every 2 rounds, enemies will receive 1 stack of
    [Incorporeal]
    .
    • If Necrologist lose all
      [Incorporeal]
      stacks, she will gain 2 more
      [Moribund]
      stacks.
  • At the first round and at the start of every 2 rounds, all allies get
    [Silence]
    for 1 round.

Strategy and tips

  • Only focus on dealing DMG to Necrologist, use single-target skills instead of multi-target skills.
  • Attack and Channel incantation will operate more efficiently, since they are not affected by
    [Silence]
    .
  • You can choose any Myth Manifests other than Operatic Reflection based on your team, but do not use Ultimate.

Silence

Control

Cannot cast Buff, Debuff, Heal, or Counter-type incantations.

Moribund

Special

Survives when taking a lethal attack, recover (Max HP x5%) HP (-1 stack after trigger, stackable, undispellable).

Incorporeal

Pos Status

Dodge 1 attack (-1 stack after trigger).

Flinch

Neg Status

DMG Taken Reduction -1%, Genesis DMG Taken +1% (stackable, undispellable).

Corrode

Neg Status

When a round ends, takes Genesis DMG based on the carrier’s current HP.

Glean

Pos Status

Genesis DMG Dealt +5% (up to 50 stacks, undispellable).

Disconcert

Neg Status

Cannot gain Moxie.
