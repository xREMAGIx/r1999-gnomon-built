Stage info

  • There are Moldir and enemies, you just need to defeat Moldir to pass the stage.
  • At the end of each round, Moldir will perform an additional attack to the highest ATK ally and make that ally fall into
    [Disarm]
    status.
  • When being attacked in [Counter] status, Moldir will riposte and also inflict
    [Disarm]
    on the attacker.
  • Moldir’s Ultimate will grant herself Critical Rate +100% and Critical DMG +50% for 3 rounds.
  • There are enemies call Shieldbearer that will be in
    [Taunt]
    status to protect Moldir from being attacked.

Strategy and tips

  • Prioritize Beast or single-target units.
  • You should bring units with dispel skills to dispel [Pos Status] and [Counter] from enemies.
  • You can deal with Shieldbearer first to remove them from protecting Moldir.
  • You can choose units have skills that are not [Attack] type skills and let them have the highest ATK to not be affected by
    [Disarm]
    .
  • Characters like Lopera should not be used to deal with Moldir since they are affected by
    [Disarm]
    , which will cause them to lose ability to perform their Follow-up attacks.
  • The best Myth Manifest to use is Operatic Reflection since it has stronger Afflatus, provides effects to Beast units.
