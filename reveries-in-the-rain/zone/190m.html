Stage info

  • There is only 1 enemy, Dikke, with high HP, DMG and [Control] immunity.
  • Every attacks of Dikke will increases her ATK or Leech Rate based on the difference between Dikke’s HP and target’s, higher or lower.
  • Every 4 rounds, Dikke will create an
    [Array]
    that increases her ATK and Leech Rate, also reduce DMG Taken.
  • When in
    [Array]
    , Dikke will also deals DMG to the far left ally at the end of the round.

Strategy and tips

  • Bring 1 ally who can create
    [Array]
    to override Dikke’s
    [Array]
    . For example, some allies below:
    • Lopera
    • Tuesday
    • Getian
    • Semmelweis
  • If you don’t have a character who can create
    [Array]
    , bring sustain ally and try to keep HP higher than Dikke, prevent her from increasing her ATK.
    • When Dikke is in
      [Array]
      , she will attack the leftmost ally, so place your best tankiest ally or unit thats not disturbed by
      [Disarm]
      like healer in the first slot to optimize sustain.
  • Pick DPS characters who can deals high DMG to single target.
  • You can choose any Myth Manifests based on your team.
