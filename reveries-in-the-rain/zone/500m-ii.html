Stress Behavior

When the Energy Analyst gains new energy, the first acquired energy will overflow. Energy overflow increases the Energy Analyst’s Stress.
When Stress is between 30 - 69, the Energy Analyst gets a significant decrease in damage taken.
When Stress is between 70 - 99, the Energy Analyst gets a significant increase in damage dealt.
When Stress reaches 100, the Energy Analyst will overload, cannot gain Stress buffs and gets a significant increase in damage taken from Ultimate for 1 round.
