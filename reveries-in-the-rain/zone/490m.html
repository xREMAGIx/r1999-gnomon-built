Primary Target

Special

Priority target for enemy attacks.

Choreographed Sequence

Stats Up

Accumulate enough stacks and consume the stacks to enhance the next skill cast.

Replay

Summons one of the player’s characters; the character is immune to all statuses, and all of the character’s damage taken is set to 1. During this period, the true form character will enter the [Still Frame] status; on death, dispel [Still Frame] from all enemies.

Wrap Up

After reaching 5 stacks, the next Skill 1 cast will additionally inflict the Instadeath effect.

NG Shot

1-target attack. Deals (the target’s Max HP x200%) DMG to the target.

Focus

Pos Status

Focus Chance +5% (stackable).

Collection of Neg Status

There are 5 effects:
[Gash]
,
[Pollution]
,
[Poison]
,
[Corrode]
, and
[Bleed]
. Prioritizes effects not yet applied.

Gash

Neg Status

When attacked, take additional (own ATK x20%) Genesis DMG.

Pollution

Neg Status

After taking action, takes (the user’s ATK x20%) Genesis DMG. (Can stack.)

Poison

Neg Status

At the end of the round, takes Genesis DMG based on the user’s ATK (stackable).

Corrode

Neg Status

When a round ends, takes Genesis DMG based on the carrier’s current HP.

Bleed

Neg Status

After action, takes (the caster’s ATK x100%) Genesis DMG.

Form and Intent

Pos Status

When being attacked, DMG Taken -50% for the attack, casts Flowing Needle on the attacker, and gains Moxie +1 for self.

Qi Stagnation

Cannot gain ultimate when held; when in the Spelldock, whenever taking an action, the holder loses (Current HP x10%) HP.
