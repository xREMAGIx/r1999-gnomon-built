Stage info

  • There are enemies called Olivery.
  • Whenever an enemy is being attacked, the ally who attacked the enemy gets 3 stack of
    [Pollution]
    .
  • If our ally do multi-target attack, the amount of
    [Pollution]
    stacks will be significantly increased.
  • Every attacks of Olivery will also inflict
    [Pollution]
    .
  • Enemies will have multi-target DMG Taken -50% and single-target DMG Taken +50%.
  • At the end of the round, if enemy’s HP is below 40%, a new enemy will spawn, can trigger 2 times, with maximum of 4 enemies on the battlefield.

Strategy and tips

  • Prioritize units with single-target attacks, since they will deal more DMG, and recommended to bring multiple sustain units to withstand
    [Pollution]
    .
  • If our allies have multi-target attacks, they will need someone to protect, recovery or remove
    [Pollution]
    on them.
  • We need to calculate so that the HP of all enemies is equal, then deal with all of them within 1 round so they cannot spawn new enemies.
  • You can choose any Myth Manifests based on your team.
