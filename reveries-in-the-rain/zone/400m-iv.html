Enervate

Neg Status

Incantation Might -10% (stackable and is removed after the Ultimate is cast).

Petrify

Control

Cannot act. Dispels the effect after taking Reality DMG attack.

Protection of Darkness

Pos Status

Genesis DMG Taken -50%. When being attacked by a basic incantation, temporary DMG Taken Reduction +80%. This effect lasts for 4 rounds, and is undispellable. While this effect is active, after being attacked by 3 ultimates in total, this effect is dispelled.

Havoc

Pos Status

Penetration Rate +5% (stacks up to 5 times).
