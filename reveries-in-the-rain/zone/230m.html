Stage info

  • There are 2 enemies, Butcher and Bubble, you just need to defeat Bubble to pass the stage.
  • After Butcher cast Ultimate, he will in
    [Taunt]
    status for 1 round.

Strategy and tips

  • Focus DMG on Bubble.
  • Butcher can make a significant amount of DMG to our allies, so allies need to be sustained well to survive.
  • When Butcher is in
    [Taunt]
    status, limit the attacks to enemies and focus on preparing for dealing DMG in the next round. Then in the next round, perform Burst DMG on Bubble.
  • Repeat this process until Bubble is dead.
  • You can choose any Myth Manifests based on your team.
