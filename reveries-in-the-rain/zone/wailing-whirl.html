Unsparing

Pos Status

DMG Dealt +5% (up to 50 stacks, undispellable).

Over-Hallucination

Special

Final DMG Reduction -50% (stackable).

Moxie Guard

Pos Status

Gains immunity to Moxie reduction.

Mind Derailment

Special

After being attacked by a non-ultimate attack, Moxie -1.

Projected Zeal

Special

After being attacked by an ultimate, Moxie -3.

Mental Rigidity

Neg Status

DMG Taken Reduction -7.5%. Genesis DMG Taken +7.5%. At the end of the round, if [Mental Rigidity] on self reaches 8 stacks, loses all stacks of [Mental Rigidity] and inflicts
[Petrify]
on a random enemy for 1 round, then loses (Current HP x5%) HP (stackable up to 8 times).

Petrify

Control

Cannot act. Dispels the effect after taking Reality DMG attack.

Burn

Neg Status

Healing Taken -15% (unstackable). At the end of the round, takes (the holder’s ATK x4%) Genesis DMG (stackable up to 30 times, multiple stacks of Burn is regarded as 1 [Neg Status], removes 50% of the stacks when triggered).

Reprise

Special

At the start of the round, dispels all stacks of [Reprise] from self after triggering any of the following effects: When the number of [Reprise] stacks is below 8 or over 12, gains Eureka +1. When the number of [Reprise] stacks is between 8 and 12, deals (Current HP x8%) DMG to self.

Daze

Control

Cannot act

Discard

After playing an incantation, discard 1 incantation (cannot discard ultimates).

Lockdown

Can only be played when in the leftmost or rightmost positions of the Spelldock (the Spelldock is also known as the “incantation area,” or your “hand”).

Dream Visit

Pos Status

Can only cast ultimates and special incantations. The holder’s automatically replenished incantations are converted into random incantations of other allies. At the start of each round, gain Moxie +1 and 1 precast incantation
[Counting Sheep]
.

Counting Sheep

After incantations are cast and queued, grant rank +1 to the incantation ahead of this incantation, and rank -1 to the incantation behind this incantation.

Poison

Neg Status

After a round ends, takes (the caster’s ATK x30%) Genesis DMG. Can stack.

Ceremonious

Special

Ultimate Might +3% (up to 100 stacks, undispellable, disappears after casting an Ultimate).

Deadline

Special

At the end of the round, deals (the holder’s current HP x33%) Genesis DMG. When targeted by a Heal/Buff/Counter/Channel incantation, dispels 1 stack of this status (stackable up to 4 times, each stack is timed independently).

Poison

Neg Status

At the end of the round, takes Genesis DMG based on the user’s ATK (stackable).

Rapture Whirlpool

Counter

When being attacked, ripostes with a mass attack against all enemies and deals 210% Mental DMG to them, but gains Incantation Might -10% for 2 rounds (stackable).
