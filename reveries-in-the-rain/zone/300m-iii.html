Stage info

  • There is only 1 enemy: Anjo Nala.
  • In the beginning of the battle, Anjo Nala already have 5 Moxie to cast Ultimate.
    • When casting Ultimate, she will summon another enemy.
    • New enemy combine with Anjo Nala will make the amount of DMG our allies receive is extremely large.
    • When in channeling status of Ultimate, she will consume Moxie and deal Mass attack to our team.
  • When our ally use Ultimate, all enemies will gain 1 Moxie.
  • When Anjo Nala is in channeling status, if she receive single-target attack, she will lose 1 Moxie.
  • If the summoned enemy is defeated, Anjo Nala will lose 5 Moxie.

Strategy and tips

  • If there are Anjo Nala and the summoned enemy, focus on attacking the summoned enemy first.
  • Bring sustain units to withstand attacks from Anjo Nala and the summoned enemy.
  • Can bring units with ability to reduce Moxie or with
    [Seal]
    ,
    [Nightmare]
    effect to prevent Anjo Nala from casting Ultimate. Focus on using these reduce Moxie skills on the round that Anjo Nala prepares to cast Ultimate.
  • Ideally, bring 1 DPS unit, 1 sustain unit and 2 units who can prevent Ultimate.
  • Try to make every ally perform at least 1 action in the round to limit receiving
    [Crippling Weakness]
    ,
    [Flinch]
    effects.
  • You can choose any Myth Manifests based on your team
    • Using Operatic Reflection will gain more Moxie that helps prevent Ultimate.
    • With Mountain Ghost, it helps rank up incantations, useful for allies who can reduce Moxie with rank 2/3 incantations.
