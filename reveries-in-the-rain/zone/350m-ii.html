Pollution

Neg Status

After taking action, takes (the user’s ATK x20%) Genesis DMG. (Can stack.)

Word Suppression

Neg Status

When reaching 4 stacks, converts to
[Silence]
for 2 rounds.

Silence

Control

Cannot cast Buff, Debuff, Heal, or Counter-type incantations.

Flinch

Neg Status

DMG Taken Reduction -1%, Genesis DMG Taken +1% (stackable, undispellable).

Bleed

Neg Status

After action, takes (the caster’s ATK x100%) Genesis DMG.

Daze

Control

Cannot act

Cursed Grudge

Neg Status

At the end of the round, the holder loses 50% of Max HP (undispellable).

Bloody Hunt

Counter

When attacked, casts 1 Brain Surgery incantation on the source of the damage. (Decreases by 1 stack after taking effect.)

Taunt

Counter

Draws the enemy’s attention. When taking certain attacks, becomes the priority of the attacks.

Poison

Neg Status

At the end of the round, takes Genesis DMG based on the user’s ATK (stackable).

Corrode

Neg Status

When a round ends, takes Genesis DMG based on the carrier’s current HP.

Gash

Neg Status

When attacked, take additional (own ATK x20%) Genesis DMG.

Seal

Control

Cannot use Ultimate.
