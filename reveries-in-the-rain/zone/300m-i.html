Stage info

  • Whenever ally use Ultimate, enter
    [Dream Visit]
    status for 1 round so use normal skills first before using Ultimate to avoid wasting skills.
  • Our team will have 1 more character:
    Barbara
  • Every time a teammate use a rank 1/2/3 skills, Barbara will receive 1/2/3 stacks of [Trouble Sleep].
    • When Barbara has 20 stacks of [Trouble Sleep], she will cast Ultimate and receive 3 Precast Incantations deal Genesis DMG that can deal significant DMG to enemies, 1 Precast Incantation for rank up incantation.

Strategy and tips

  • Some character does not fit in this zone. For example:
    • Windsong
      after using Ultimate will be immobile immediately and can not perform Follow-up attacks.
  • Can just need to bring sustain units to withstand, use Barbara Precast Incantations to deal DMG to enemies to pass this zone.
  • You can choose any Myth Manifests based on your team.
