Stage info

  • There are Semmelweis and other enemies.
  • During the battle, enemies will receive an effect called
    [Moribund]
    .
  • Our team will have 1 more character:
    Mondlicht
  • Ultimate of Mondlicht will dispel
    [Moribund]
    on all enemies.
  • In the first round, Semmelweis, in Ultimate status, has ability to deals large DMG to all allies.
  • Phase 2 will have almost the same mechanism as Phase 1.

Strategy and tips

  • Bring units with these traits to withstand first attacks of Semmelweis or prevent her from Ultimate in the first round:
    • Who can sustain or create
      [Shield]
      .
    • Who has [Control], reduce Moxie skills.
    • Who can create
      [Array]
      to override Semmelweis’s
      [Array]
      .
  • In the round that Mondlicht can cast Ultimate, use Mondlicht’s Ultimate to dispel
    [Moribund]
    on all enemies first, then use attacks skills to deal DMG and finish off enemies.
  • You can choose any Myth Manifests based on your team.

Enervate

Neg Status

Incantation Might -10% (stackable and is removed after the Ultimate is cast).

Blood Siphon

Neg Status

Max HP -5% (stacks up to 10 times).

Dread

Special

DMG Taken Reduction -5%; at the end of the round, deal (the holder’s current HP x3%) Genesis DMG. When targeted by a healing, buff, counter, or channel incantation, dispel 1 stack of this status. At the end of the round, if this status has reached 10 stacks, additionally lose 90% of current HP (stackable up to 10 times).

Regen

Pos Status

At the start of each round, recover (own Max HP x1%) HP (up to 50 stacks, undispellable).

Moribund

Special

Survives when taking a lethal attack, recover (Max HP x5%) HP (-1 stack after trigger, stackable, undispellable).

Chaos

Neg Status

Incantations may select the wrong target.

Nightmare

Control

Cannot act. Dispels the effect after taking any attack.

Fresh Blood

Special

ATK +30% and Leech Rate +30%; after attacking, inflicts 1 stack of
[Blood Siphon]
on the main target.

Blood Domain

Whenever an ally attacks, for every 10% of their Missing HP, gain an additional DMG Bonus +10%.

Feral Instinct

Pos Status

At the end of the round, casts
[Truth Revealed]
if still under this status.

Truth Revealed

Mass attack. Deals 300% Reality DMG to all enemies. This attack gains Leech Rate +100% and Moxie +1.

Terror

Neg Status

When a round ends, Moxie -1.

Shield

Shield

Blocks certain DMG for a certain duration.

Array

Battlefield effect. Continuously affects characters on the field (only one type of array can exist at a time, whether on the allied or enemy side).
