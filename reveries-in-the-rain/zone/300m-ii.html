Stage info

  • Enemies are the main boss Hopeless Drifter and other critters, you just need to defeat Hopeless Drifter to pass the stage.
  • At the end of each round, Hopeless Drifter will inflict
    [Petrify]
    on random ally.
    • They also have skills that can inflict
      [Petrify]
      .
  • The main boss can also summon other critters
    • With Beast critter, be careful when this unit attack, since it can deal a lot of DMG.
    • With Green Lake critter, it will inflict
      [Pollution]
      on our allies when attacks or being defeated.
    • With Cactus critter, it will have
      [Taunt]
      effect.
  • When go to phase 2, the critters will be strengthened and become much stronger
    • When entering battle, they will receive a large amount of [Shield] for 2 rounds.

Strategy and tips

  • Units whose DMG depend on Ultimate will not be ideal for this zone, since after casting Ultimate, they will get permanent Ultimate Might -10%.
  • Bring units with dispel skills to dispel
    [Petrify]
    .
  • When entering phase 2, in the first 2 rounds, we can skip not to attack enemies, instead focus on buff, enhance skills then deal Burst DMG in the third round.
  • You can choose any Myth Manifests based on your team.

Sturdiness

Pos Status

When attacked, DMG Taken Reduction +25% (-1 stack after trigger).

Unsparing

Pos Status

DMG Dealt +5% (up to 50 stacks, undispellable).

Pollution

Neg Status

After taking action, takes (the user’s ATK x20%) Genesis DMG. (Can stack.)

Thorny

Counter

Reflect (own ATK x15%) Genesis DMG (up to 50 stacks, undispellable).

Burn

Neg Status

Healing Taken -15% (unstackable). At the end of the round, takes (the holder’s ATK x4%) Genesis DMG (stackable up to 30 times, multiple stacks of Burn is regarded as 1 [Neg Status], removes 50% of the stacks when triggered).

Petrify

Control

Cannot act. Dispels the effect after taking Reality DMG attack.

Enhancement Ban

Neg Status

Cannot gain [Stats Up], [Pos Status], [Counter], or [Channel] statuses.

Photosynthesis

Pos Status

At the start of the round, if this channel status is still active, recover 10% Max HP for self and gain Moxie +2 (removed after being attacked by an ultimate).

Mercy

Pos Status

Healing Done +5% (stacks up to 50 times, undispellable).

Unsparing

Pos Status

DMG Dealt +5% (up to 50 stacks, undispellable).

Rigidity

Neg Status

When there are 2 stacks, turns into Petrify status or extends the duration of existing Petrify status for 1 round.

Poison

Neg Status

At the end of the round, takes Genesis DMG based on the user’s ATK (stackable).

Corrode

Neg Status

When a round ends, takes Genesis DMG based on the carrier’s current HP.

Taunt

Counter

Draws the enemy’s attention. When taking certain attacks, becomes the priority of the attacks.

Lucky

Pos Status

Critical Rate +5%, Critical DMG +5% (up to 50 stacks, undispellable).

Glean

Pos Status

Genesis DMG Dealt +5% (up to 50 stacks, undispellable).

Seal

Control

Cannot use Ultimate.
