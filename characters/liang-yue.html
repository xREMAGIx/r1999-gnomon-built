Overview

Liang Yue is a character that dealt expressive DMG through [Talon] and [Quiangliang] mechanism.

With Special buff
[Arise, Qiangliang!]
:
  • In Spelldock will appear 2 zones at the start of next round:
    • Green zone on the left side, including 3 incantations.
    • Red zone on the right side, including 3 incantations.
When using incantations in the Red zone:
  • Incantation will have rank +1 before casting.
  • Gain 1 [Justice Talon].
When using incantations in the Green zone:
  • [Stats Up], [Stats Down], [Counter], [Pos Status], [Neg Status] from incantation will gain +1 round duration.
  • Gain 1 [Peace Talon].
When using incantations in the overlap of these 2 zones:
  • Receive both +1 rank and +1 round duration.
  • Gain 0.5 [Peace Talon] and 0.5 [Justice Talon].
Special incantations still can grant [Talon] without consumed AP, for example: Versatile incantation of Vila
When Liang Yue gain enough 6 stacks of [Talon] she will dealt a Follow-up Attack called [Bane of All Evil].

[Bane of All Evil]
will be cast differently depending on the balance of [Justice Talon] and [Peace Talon]:
  • If there’s more [Justice Talon], cast [Bane of All Evil - Justice].
  • If there’s more [Peace Talon], cast [Bane of All Evil - Peace].
  • If [Justice Talon] and [Peace Talon] are equal, cast [Bane of All Evil - Balance].
  • The amount of exceed [Talon] will be counted for the next [Bane of All Evil].
  • For each [Talon] consumed, grant 1 stack of
    [Guardian's Resolve]
    to all allies for 2 rounds.

First, with
[Bane of All Evil · Justice]
:
  • Deal 400% AoE Reality DMG.
  • Considered as a rank 2 incantation.
  • For each [Justice Talon] consumed, this attack will have Critical Rate +5%, Critical DMG +20%, and DMG Dealt +10%
  • Excess Critical Rate is converted into Critical DMG.

Next, with
[Bane of All Evil · Peace]
:
  • Deal 400% AoE Reality DMG.
  • For each [Peace Talon] consumed, before attack, target will receive 1 stack of
    [Poison]
    for 1 round and 3 stacks of
    [Burn]
    .
  • For each [Poison] stack on target, this attack will have DMG Dealt +10%.
  • For each [Burn] stack on target, this attack will have Penetration Rate +3%.

And with
[Bane of All Evil · Balance]
:
  • Deal 200% AoE Reality DMG.
  • Grant Liang Yue 1 stack of
    [Swift Resolve]
    • This is a Special buff.
    • Maximum stacks is 2.
    • Reduce 1 stack of [Talon] that required to cast [Bane of All Evil].
    • Increase 20% Reality DMG for [Bane of All Evil - Justice]/[Bane of All Evil - Peace], remove after trigger.
    • Increase up to 60% (20% for all type of [Bane of All Evil] + 40%) Reality DMG for [Bane of All Evil - Balance], not remove after trigger.
    • It will also be removed when [Arise, Qiangliang!] end.

With first skill
[Banish Evil]
  • Deal single target Reality DMG.
  • At rank 2/3, this attack will have additional 30%/80% Penetration Rate.
  • Extends the duration of
    [Guardian's Resolve]
    on all allies by 1 round.
    • This will not have +1 round duration even when in the Green zone.
    • If this skill is casted twice in a row, the duration will be increased by 2 rounds.
  • When in the [Arise, Qiangliang!] status, gain 1 additional [Justice Talon].

The second incantation
[Bless Life]
  • Single target Buff.
  • Grant
    [Guardian's Blessing I]
    /
    [Guardian's Blessing II]
    /
    [Guardian's Blessing III]
    for 1 round.
    • If the buff receiver attacking/being attacked/at the end of the round, cast [Banish Evil] as a Follow-up Attack on the target/the attacker/the enemy with the lowest HP.
    • This attack also have all the effect as the first skill, but it will not gain 1 [Justice Talon] since it’s not actively casting.
    • If the buff receiver being attacked, this attack will fall to [Riposte] category and also receive buff for [Riposte] type.
  • When casting in the Green zone, above effect will also have +1 round duration.
  • When in the [Arise, Qiangliang!] status, gain 1 additional [Peace Talon].
  • If Liang Yue can not trigger Follow-up Attack, this buff still be reserved.
  • This incantation at rank 1/2/3 can be casted independently.

Ultimate skill
[Call of Thunder: #0305]
  • Deal AoE Reality DMG
  • Grants 1 stack of
    [Immunity]
    to all allies for 1 rounds.
  • Grants 6 stacks of
    [Guardian's Resolve]
    to all allies for 2 rounds.
  • When casting Ultimate in the Green zone, above effects will also have +1 round duration.
  • Grants or extend (if already in this status) [Arise, Qiangliang!] to self for 3 rounds.

Operate / Rotation
At Portray 0, because Liang Yue can only have Red and Green zone for 2 rounds only (since zones take effect at the start of next round), so the rotation would be difficult to maintain. If there’s no zone, her DMG will be reduced significantly.
Some ways to solve this is to have Anjo Nala in the team or Liang Yue must be at Portray 1, so the 3-rounds rotation can be ensured.
  • If Liang Yue is bound with Anjo Nala, when Anjo Nala’s ultimate is casted, Liang Yue’s ultimate will also be triggered before the start of the next round, so Red, Green zone will be available for total 3 rounds.
  • If Liang Yue is at Portray 1, [Arise, Qiangliang!] will be extended to 4 rounds, means both zones will last for 3 rounds.

When operate Liang Yue, you need to pay attention to:
  • Maintain Ultimate
  • Use suitable incantations in Red and Green zone

Use Buff skill
[Bless Life]
on Liang Yue to gain the optimized DMG Dealt.
  • Try using this skill right before [Bane of All Evil], and after any attack skill so the DMG Dealt Bonus can be applied to [Bane of All Evil].
When Liang Yue is in
[Arise, Qiangliang!]
  • [Banish Evil]
    will gain 1 [Justice Talon].
  • [Bless Life]
    will gain 1 [Peace Talon].
If these skills are casted in Red, Green zone, [Talon] from incantation effect will take effect first, then accumulate additional [Talon] from zones.

Cast to gain Moxie enough to Ultimate. Prioritize Buff skill
[Bless Life]
Cast Ultimate to enter [Arise, Qiangliang!] status.
Use these skill every round (prioritize buff skill) with other character skills to recast Ultimate to maintain [Arise, Qiangliang!] status

Both [Bane of All Evil - Justice] and [Bane of All Evil - Peace] have almost the same DMG Dealt.
[Bane of All Evil - Justice] will:
  • Harder to play than [Bane of All Evil - Peace]
  • Have larger DMG Dealt when have Flutterpage in the team.
  • Not be countered when meet enemy with [Neg Status] resistance.
[Bane of All Evil - Peace] will:
  • Easier to play than [Bane of All Evil - Justice]
  • Have larger DMG Dealt when do not have Flutterpage in the team.
  • Be countered when meet enemy with [Neg Status] resistance.
[Bane of All Evil - Balance] is not always recommended because DMG Dealt is lower than the other ones.
So pay attention on which incantation to play in Red and Green zone to trigger the appropriate [Bane of All Evil].

Guardian's Resolve

Stats Up

DMG Taken -0.8%, (stackable up to 30 times, each stack is timed independently).

Arise, Qiangliang!

Special

Enhances and categorizes the allied Spelldock into different sections, granting bonuses to incantations based on the section the incantations are played from: The 3 rightmost incantations played from the Spelldock gain rank +1 before resolve; The 3 leftmost incantations played from the Spelldock gain +1 round duration to the
[Specified Selection]
effects that the incantations actively apply. The incantations played from the overlap section of the Spelldock gain both of the bonus effects above.

Specified Selection

[Stats Up], [Stats Down], [Counter], [Pos Status], [Neg Status].

Justice Talon

While in the
[Arise, Qiangliang!]
