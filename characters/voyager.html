Confusion

Neg Status

Critical Resist -25% (can stack; each stack is timed independently).

Interstellar Blessing

Counter

Inflicts Genesis DMG on the attacker equal to 100% of Voyager’s ATK (reduced by 1 stack on trigger).

Sturdiness

Pos Status

When attacked, DMG Taken Reduction +25% (-1 stack after trigger).

Refrain

Pos Status

If there are enemies after the caster takes action, inflicts 1 round of
[Silence]
on all enemies (cannot be dispelled).

Silence

Control

Cannot cast Buff, Debuff, Heal, or Counter-type incantations.

Seal

Control

Cannot use Ultimate.

Inspiration

For every 1 Eureka consumed, accumulate 1 point of [Inspiration]. At the start of each round, randomly distribute Inspiration points onto basic incantations (each incantation may receive up to 5 points of Inspiration, and at the end of the round, the points of Inspiration on unused incantations are cleared). When actively casting an incantation with [Inspiration] points on it, add an
[Impromptu Incantation]
to the end of the spell queue, and all Inspiration points on these casted incantations will be granted to the
[Impromptu Incantation]
.

Impromptu Incantation

1-target attack. Deals 200% Mental DMG to a random enemy and an additional (the total points of Inspiration granted to Impromptu Incantation x15%) Mental DMG. If Impromptu Incantation has at least 3/6/12/20 points of Inspiration, it gains attack instances +1/2/3/5 (Impromptu Incantation’s DMG calculation is based on the average stats of all allies).

Where the Stars Lead

Neg Status

Impromptu Incantations target this unit with priority. When being attacked by an Impromptu Incantation, takes X Genesis DMG, where X is equal to ((the user’s ATK x (50% + the additional Mental DMG % dealt by [Galaxy on the Strings] x 50%)); triggers up to 5 times per round.
