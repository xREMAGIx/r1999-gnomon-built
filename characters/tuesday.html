Overview

Tuesday is one of the key character in the poison quad with the strong interaction with
[Poison]
effect.

All the
[Poison]
cause by Tuesday will deal Genesis DMG to the target scale by 20% of Tuesday’s ATK, which is lower than most [Poison] characters (30%).
With Insight 1, gains
[Fear's Cradle]
, that make all
[Poison]
become Critical hits.
  • If ally inflicts any [Stats Down], [Neg Status], or [Control] status, it has a 50% chance to inflict 1 instance of
    [Poison]
    on that target for 1 round, based on ally’s ATK.
  • This inflict effect will be calculated separately, which means more [Stats Down], [Neg Status], or [Control] status, more chances to inflict
    [Poison]
    .
  • Only active inflict actions will trigger this effect.
Insight 3 grant Tuesday
[Room Service]
, help exceed Moxie on allies can be temporarily stored.
  • At the end of the round, these stored Moxie on allies can be used to recharge the missing Moxie.
  • Each character can store up to 5 Moxie.

With first skill
[The Horror’s Delight]
  • Deals DMG and inflicts 2 stacks of
    [Poison]
    on the target and 1 stack on another random legal enemy target, repeat 2-4 times based on rank.
  • If there is only 1 enemy left, it will only inflict 2 stacks of
    [Poison]
    on the target.

[In Mother’s Arms]
is a multi target Debuff skill
  • Increase Genesis DMG Taken and reduce Critical Resist Rate on all enemies for 2 rounds.
  • It’s recommended to use this incantation every 2 rounds to maintain the Debuff.

Ultimate
[Please, Come On In]
  • Deals DMG to all enemies, and inflicts 1 stack of
    [Poison]
    .
  • Deals additional Generis DMG based on the number of instances of
    [Poison]
    on the target, and they can be Critical hits.
  • This skill will also create an
    [Array]
    :
    [Horror Story Night]
    in 3 rounds.
  • [Poison]
    during this [Array] time will not be decreased in duration.
  • At the start of each round when the [Array] is available,
    [Poison]
    effect will trigger 1 more time.
  • When the [Array] ends or is replaced,
    [Poison]
    will be resolved on all enemies.

Poison

Neg Status

At the end of the round, takes Genesis DMG based on the user’s ATK (stackable).

Horror Story Night

[Array]
effect:
[Poison]
effects on enemies do not decrease in duration on resolve. At the start of the enemy’s round, resolve 1 round of their
[Poison]
effects. After the array ends or is replaced, immediately resolve
[Poison]
on all enemies.

Array

Battlefield effect. Continuously affects characters on the field (only one type of array can exist at a time, whether on the allied or enemy side).

Fear's Cradle

Pos Status

All allies can score critical hits with their
[Poison]
effects. When an ally takes an action, every [Stats Down], [Neg Status], or [Control] status the ally inflicts on an enemy target through this action has a 50% chance to inflict 1 instance of
[Poison]
on that target for 1 round: at the end of the round, deals (the user’s ATK x20%) Genesis DMG to the target (undispellable).

Room Service

Pos Status

Excess Moxie on allies is temporarily stored. At the end of the round, each character’s stored Moxie is used to replenish their own Moxie if able. Each character can store up to 5 points of Moxie (undispellable).
