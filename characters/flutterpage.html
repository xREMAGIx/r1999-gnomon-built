Overview

Flutterpage is a character that buff Follow-up attack (FuA) characters through the
[Gust]
effect.
[Gust]
can be gained by:
  • Other allies actions (active or FuA) will grant 1 stack last 3 rounds.
  • Casting
    [Knock, Knock]
    will grant 2 stacks last 3 rounds.
  • 10 stacks of
    [Gust]
    last 2 rounds when entering battle when updated to Insight 3

At the beginning of each round, based on the stacks of
[Gust]
Flutterpage has, she will grant a
[Force Field]
that benefits all allies and the added Reality DMG is considered dealt by Flutterpage herself:
  • Up to 5 stacks or more: gain
    [Paper Parade]
    .
  • Up to 15 stacks or more: gain
    [Wave of Sheets]
    .
  • Up to 25 stacks or more: gain
    [Adieu, Mother Earth]
    .
To maintain
[Force Field]
level 3, it is recommended to make allies do extra actions 2-3 times per round.

Flutterpage has 8 Eureka in total.
[Force Field]
at level 2 and above doesn’t need to consume Eureka and still benefits all allies but will lose some DMG.
With Insight 3, when Flutterpage attacks will grant 2 Eureka to self.
When in Ultimate channeling, Flutterpage will recover all Eureka at the start of each round.

[Cotton Kite]
is a skill that grant a
[Continuous Action I]
effect for 1 round, which takes effect in the following round.
So prioritize to use this skill to gain Moxie and
[Gust]
stacks for Flutterpage.
[Continuous Action I]
effect is not stackable so don’t use it twice in a round and don’t combine them to 2 or 3 stars as it only increases Reality DMG.

Ultimate skill
[A Dream That Never Grows Old]
will recover all Eureka and convert all incantations into
[Regards from the Sky]
as a Follow-up Attack at the start of each round.
At the end of each round, Flutterpage will cast 1
[Regards from the Sky]
additionally.
Since this will convert all incantations into
[Regards from the Sky]
, despite 1/2/3 stars, so it not recommended to combine incantations of Flutterpage.
Moxie will not be gained during the Ultimate channeling. At the end of channeling, Moxie will be recovered based on the current level of
[Force Field]

Operate / Rotation

  • Always use
    [Cotton Kite]
    when it is in Spelldock, but only 1 per round.
  • Use
    [A Dream That Never Grows Old]
    ultimate to enter channeling status.
  • [Knock, Knock]
    is not prioritized, only use when you need to gain Moxie to Ultimate.

[Cotton Kite]
Cast Ultimate to enter channeling status for 3 rounds, can cast
[Cotton Kite]
in between to gain AP in next round
When channeling status end, cast these skill to gain Moxie to Ultimate again.
