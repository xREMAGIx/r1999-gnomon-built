Serpentine Scales

Special

At the end of the round, take ((50% + (number of stacks x 20)%) x Hissabeth’s ATK) Genesis DMG. This effect can critically hit and benefits from Ultimate Might bonuses. After each trigger of this effect, 1 stack of Serpentine Scales is converted into 1 stack of
[Snake's Den]
and granted to Hissabeth. When the holder of this status dies, the remaining stacks of [Serpentine Scales] are transferred onto the ally with the highest ATK. This status can stack up to 8 times and is undispellable.

Snake's Den

Special

A place where the siblings nestle. This status can stack up to 8 times and is undispellable. At the start of the round, this unit adjusts the stacks of [Snake’s Den] on self so that the combined total of [Snake’s Den], [Serpentine Scales], and [Hiss!] on the field equals 8.

Hiss!

1-target attack. Deals 50%/100%/250% Mental DMG; inflicts 1/2/4 stacks of
[Serpentine Scales]
and gains 0/1/2 Moxie. This effect counts as an ultimate and does not consume AP.

Rigidity

Neg Status

When there are 2 stacks, turns into Petrify status or extends the duration of existing Petrify status for 1 round.
