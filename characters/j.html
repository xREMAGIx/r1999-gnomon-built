Overview

J is a Shield, DPS and
[Burn]
effect oriented character.
Not only applying [Burn] to enemies, he also apply them to himself to boost DMG and reduce his own HP.

All of J’s attacks can apply [Burn] to enemies, from basic incatations to Ultimate, or counterattacks.
If J has
[Preignition]
buff, the amount of [Burn] from this can also be converted to J’s [Burn].
In Insight 1
  • Every times J inflicts [Burn] on an enemy, he grants one stack of [Burn] to self.
  • Everytime J attacks, the amount of [Burn] he has will be triggered immediately without waiting till the end of the round.
  • If J counterattacks continuously, each attack activate [Burn] will cause J to lose HP continuously.
  • For each stack of [Burn] on self, J will also gain ATK percentage equal to half the number of stacks of [Burn] on self.
  • Take 50% DMG from [Burn] and never fall below 15% HP.
With Insight 3
  • J will immune to
    [Freeze]
    and
    [Cold]
    while in the [Burn] status.
  • The [Burn] effect applied to himself can be up to 35 stacks (common [Burn] only have maximum 30 stacks).
  • At the start of the round, if J is below 50% HP, dispel all non-Burn [Stats Down] and [Neg Status] from self, then grant Critical Rate +20% to self for 1 round.

First skill
[Hardness Testing]
is a single target Debuff.
  • Deals DMG to target and inflict 2 stacks of [Burn].
  • For each stack of [Burn] on self, grant all allies DMG Dealt +1% for 2 rounds.
  • The amount of DMG Bonus will be fixed at the time of skill casted.

With
[Heat Treating]
incantation
  • Provide allies with a
    [Shield]
    , scales with J’s ATK for 2 rounds.
  • For each stack of [Burn] on self, grants an additional 3% Shield Factor.
  • If [Burn] is triggered during J’s attacks, from the effect of Insight 1, it will reduce his HP.
  • Otherwise, the [Burn] activated at the end of the round will have its DMG reduced by the [Shield].
  • When the shielded allies are attacked, J ripostes with
    [Tempering]
    .
  • The number of ripostes is equal to the amount of allies who have J’s [Shield] and being attacked by enemies.
  • The ripostes is considered as a Follow up Attack.
  • If J’s [Shield] is present and another [Shield] is applied, that [Shield] will replace J’s [Shield] if it has more HP than the previous one (with Ms. NewBabel’s Euphoria, she has the ability to increase the HP of old [Shield], which can work around this).

For the Ultimate
[Over the Haight Street!]
  • Deals DMG and apply the amount of [Burn] on self to all enemies.
  • After applying [Burn], for each stacks exceeds 30, the DMG will be increased by 10%.
  • The exceed [Burn] stacks will only be calculated based on the chosen target, so choose the enemy with the highest [Burn] stacks to maximize the DMG Bonus.
  • J does not gain [Burn] in this attack.

Operate / Rotation

  • Maintaining the [Shield] and ripostes by using
    [Heat Treating]
    incantation.
  • To increase DMG Bonus, use
    [Hardness Testing]
    when J have high [Burn] stacks.
  • Wait for J and enemies to have high the [Burn] stacks, then use
    [Over the Haight Street!]
    , we do not to use it right away.

This skill should be used only when J has high [Burn] stacks
