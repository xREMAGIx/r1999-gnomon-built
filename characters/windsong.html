Preparation - Windsong

Special

At the start of the next round, generate a
[Precast Incantation]
- [Rehabilitation] (effect stackable). If there is no [Practicability] on hand, generate an additional
[Precast Incantation]
- [Practicability].

Precast Incantation

A [Precast Incantation] is regarded as an incantation of the same name. At the end of the round, all unused [Precast Incantation] are removed.

Confusion

Neg Status

Critical Resist -25% (can stack; each stack is timed independently).

Mapping

Pos Status

Cannot lose Eureka. For every excess Eureka, gain 1 stack of
[Measure]
(undispellable).

Measure

Pos Status

Critical Rate +3% and Critical DMG +3% for 2 rounds, up to 15 stacks (each stack is timed independently, undispellable).
