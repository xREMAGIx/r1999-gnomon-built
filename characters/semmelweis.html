Fresh Blood

Special

Right before actively casting an incantation, lose (Current HP x5%) HP to grant Critical Rate +15% to this action. Only 1 [Fresh Blood] can exist on the allied side at a time.

Blood Domain

[Array]
effect: Allied characters gain DMG Taken Reduction +20%. Whenever an ally attacks, for every 10% of their Missing HP, gain an additional DMG Bonus +5% (up to 40%).

Array

Battlefield effect. Continuously affects characters on the field (only one type of array can exist at a time, whether on the allied or enemy side).

Bloodtithe

Whenever allies lose 3000 HP combined, accumulate 1 point of [Bloodtithe]; HP lost from being attacked accumulates [Bloodtithe] at only 30% efficiency.

Blood Domain

[Array]
effect: All allies DMG Taken Reduction +20%, Healing Taken +10%. For every 10% of their Missing Max HP, gain DMG Bonus +5% (up to 40%) and Healing Taken +4% (up to 32%).

Fresh Blood

Special

When taking an action, loses (Current HP x10%) HP to gain Critical Rate +20% and Critical DMG +20%. Only 1 [Fresh Blood] can exist on the allied side at a time.
