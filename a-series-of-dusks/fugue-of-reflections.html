Fugue of Reflections

Fugue of Reflections is one of the expansion for “A Series of Dusks.” This expansion adds a new ending to the base game and introduces character-exclusive artefacts known as [Acoustica].

New Ending

You don’t need to have the expansion loaded up to experience the new ending, but you do need to have completed the [A Wish Granted] ending.

Acoustica Artefacts

With the expansion loaded up, you can acquire [Acoustica] artefacts during exploration. These artefacts represent exclusive power ups for certain characters, and require the character to be in specific party positions to activate their effects.
You can enhance [Acoustica] artefacts by meeting certain conditions.
By meeting all enhancement requirements, an [Acoustica] artefact becomes [Fully Enhanced]. Based on how many fully enhanced artefacts are in your layout, additional [Harmonia] buff effects can be triggered, further strengthening your team.

Harmonia buff

Harmonia effects from 1/2/4 fully enhanced [Acoustica]:
  • When entering battle, recover 5 Stimulus.
  • At the start of the round, draw 2 incantations. At the end of the round, if Incantation Dissipation was triggered this round, the ally with the lowest Moxie gains Moxie +2, triggers 2 times. (Incantation Dissipation is when excess incantations drawn beyond the incantation limit disappear).
  • AP+1
Completing the final path node of the Ring Road and defeating the [Impending Crisis] battle guarantees you one [Acoustica] artefact, prioritizing those exclusive to your current companion. You may also find [Acoustica] artefacts during exploration.

Tougher Battles

With the expansion loaded up, enemy stats are increased. During an [Impending Crisis] and the final battle, you will also gain three powerful special incantations. Once used up, however, the enemies will become significantly stronger.

Base Content

The base content has also been updated with the following:
  • New Path Node: The [Tuning Hall] path node has been added.
  • New Combat Mechanics: Some battles can now include extra detrimental incantations that are added to your hand during combat.
  • New Artefacts: [Acoustica] artefacts and [Fabled] quality artefacts have been added.
