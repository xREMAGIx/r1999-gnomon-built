Mechanism

Impromtu Incantation

[Impromptu Incantation]
is a mechanism that deal DMG based on
[Inspiration]
points.

Whenever an ally consumes Eureka, the accumulated [Inspiration] points will be displayed on the right side of the screen:
  • These points are used to randomly distribute to skill cards in the next round.
  • These points will be attached to normal skill cards, not Ultimate.
  • Each skill card can be attached up to 5 points of [Inspiration].

When using cards with [Inspiration] points, it will record the total points, then summarize them into [Impromtu Incantation], which appears at the end of the skill chain.
If the skill cards with [Inspiration] points are not used, the accumulated [Inspiration] points will be cleared at the end of the round.
When 2 skills with [Inspiration] points are combined, the points will also be added together.

[Impromtu Incantation] deals multiple random single-target attacks on enemies:
  • This is considered a separate entity.
  • It’s attacks will not be counted as active skill or Follow up Attack
  • The more points of [Inspiration] accumulated, the more times it will attack, and it will deal greater DMG, so aim to use cards with higher points or use many cards with points as much as possible.
  • Each single-target attack that deals 200% Mental DMG
  • With each point of [Inspiration], the attack deals an additional 15% Mental DMG
  • If it has at least 3/6/12/20 points of [Inspiration], it gains attack instances +1/2/3/5
For example: if it has 6 points of [Inspiration], it will deal 1 + 2 = 3 attacks, with 200% + (15% x 3) = 245% Mental DMG

[Impromtu Incantation]’ DMG calculation is based on the average stats of all allies. It will affected by stats such as:
  • ATK
  • Critical
  • Incantation Might
  • DMG Bonus
  • Penetration Rate
  • Mental DEF Reduction
There are some buffs that does not affect [Impromtu Incantation], such as buffs that activate when attacking.

Impromptu Incantation

1-target attack. Deals 200% Mental DMG to a random enemy and an additional (the total points of Inspiration granted to Impromptu Incantation x15%) Mental DMG. If Impromptu Incantation has at least 3/6/12/20 points of Inspiration, it gains attack instances +1/2/3/5 (Impromptu Incantation’s DMG calculation is based on the average stats of all allies).

Inspiration

For every 1 Eureka consumed, accumulate 1 point of [Inspiration]. At the start of each round, randomly distribute Inspiration points onto basic incantations (each incantation may receive up to 5 points of Inspiration, and at the end of the round, the points of Inspiration on unused incantations are cleared). When actively casting an incantation with [Inspiration] points on it, add an
[Impromptu Incantation]
to the end of the spell queue, and all Inspiration points on these casted incantations will be granted to the
[Impromptu Incantation]
.
