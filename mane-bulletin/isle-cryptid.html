Isle Cryptid

Mental

Stage mechanism

  • The main boss is Isle Master
  • The minions: Shepherd and Cupbearer
  • Plant afflatus units gain 20% stats increase in ATK, DEF and HP
  • Mental DMG units increase their DMG by 15%, after cast a rank 2/3 incantation, gain 1
    [Rousing Morale]
    stack.
  • When in [Shield] status, Isle Master gains Penetration Rate +30%. When attacking, if it is under 2 or more [Stats Down] or [Neg Status] status, decrease DMG Dealt by 25%.
  • When the boss is attacked or any of minions is defeated, gain [Thunderous Movement] stack
    • 1-star incantation: 1 stack
    • 2-star incantation: 3 stacks
    • 3-star incantation: 5 stacks
    • Follow-up Attack: 1 stack
    • Minion is defeated: 3 stacks
  • At the end of the round, after trigger Isle Master any of these following effect, dispel its [Thunderous Movement] stacks
    • If it has 8-13 stacks, deals a Mental DMG follow up attack to 2 units. If it is in [Shield] status, this will also have Penetration Rate increase by 50%.
    • If it has 14 or more stacks, it will cause DMG on itself equal to 20% Max HP.
  • The Shepherd (minion on the right), at the end of the round, if it hasn’t taken any DMG in the round, enters
    [Taunt]
    for 1 round, which can be dispelled by rank 2/3 incantation.
  • The Cupbearer (minion on the left), has a attack skill that inflict a status that increases DMG Bonus by 10%, but also increases DMG Taken by 30%, this status can be dispelled by rank 2/3 incantation.

Crisis Column mode

  • While Isle Master is in [Shield] status, reduces DMG Taken by 20%. When the [Shield] is broken, increases DMG Taken by 60% for 1 round.

Strategy and tips

  • Prioritize Plant & Mental DMG units since they deal more damage.
  • Using units that can cast multiple ranked incantation or has mechanics to rank up incantations to continuously attacking Isle Master with rank 2/3 incantations.
  • Prevent Shepherd from entering
    [Taunt]
    status.
