The Cocooned Lament

Mental

Stage info

Bounty Rules

[Ally] Mineral and Beast Afflatus characters’ ATK +20%, Reality DEF +20%, Mental DEF +20%, and Max HP +20%.
[Ally] Mental DMG characters gain DMG Bonus +20%. Reality DMG characters gain DMG Bonus -60%.

Obscurity: 100

  • After being attacked, Obscurity decreases. After being attacked by a Stronger Afflatus, Obscurity decreases. Immune to Control effects, which decrease Obscurity instead.
  • At 0 Obscurity, trigger a Witness Round and extra points. Obscurity fully restores after Witness Round ends.
  • After being attacked by an Extra Action, Obscurity decreases.

Strategy and tips

  • When selecting team, we should consider about these factors:
    • Mineral and Beast characters will be buffed thanks to the bounty rules.
    • Favor units with mass attack skills or extra action skills.
    • Can bring units with [Control] effect skill to inflict it on the minions
  • You should focus on killing the minions first because
    • Whenever they act, The Cocooned Lament will gain 1 stack of
      [Cocoon]
      and 1 stack of
      [Breaking Cocoon]
      .
    • If you bring character with [Control] effect skill, inflict it on the minions will grant 3 stacks of
      [Fragility]
      to The Cocooned Lament.
  • After removing all minions, you can attack The Cocooned Lament
    • If attacked by non-extra action, it will have Critical Resist Rate and Critical DEF increased.
    • After casting Ultimate, it will summon minions again, also remove all
      [Breaking Cocoon]
      stacks.
