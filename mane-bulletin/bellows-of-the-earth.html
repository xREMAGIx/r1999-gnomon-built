Bellows of the Earth

Reality

Stage mechanism

  • For both allies and enemies, whenever is attacked, reduces the DMG taken by 5% for that turn (stackable up to 5 times).
  • When allies defeat an enemy, DMG Bonus +1% (stackable up to 20 times). After 10 stacks, allies also gain 10% Penetration Rate.
  • When allies use a mass attack skill, gains DMG Bonus +30% and Leech Rate +10%.
  • When a minion is defeated, gains all enemies 2 [Build Up] stacks, and BotE will trigger

    [Energy Explosion]

    Mass attack. Deals 50% Reality DMG to all its allies and and additional DMG equal to 10% Max HP.
    .
  • For each minions alive, BotE gains Penetration Rate +10%.
  • At the end of the round, BotE gains a buff that increases DMG Bonus 5% (stacks up to 10 times), remove 1 stack when being attacked.
  • At the end of the round, if there are less than 3 minions alive, BotE will consume 6% of HP left and summon a new minion.
  • Both BotE and minions when being attacked, will gain 1 [Build Up] stack, if it’s a active action, it will gain additional 2 stacks. Upon reaching 9 stacks, BotE will trigger

    [Energy Explosion]

    Mass attack. Deals 50% Reality DMG to all its allies and and additional DMG equal to 10% Max HP.
    .

Strategy and tips

  • Prioritize units with mass attack skills since they deal more damage.
  • Kill minions to deal DMG to BotE.
  • Attack as many times as possible, since it will help enemies stack up [Build Up] and trigger

    [Energy Explosion]

    Mass attack. Deals 50% Reality DMG to all its allies and and additional DMG equal to 10% Max HP.
    .
    • Favor Follow-up attacks units.
