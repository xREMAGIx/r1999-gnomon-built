Hypothesis of Exhibition

Reality

Stage mechanism

  • The main boss is Strange Doctor, score is earned by attacking it.
  • Beast and Plant afflatus units gain 20% stats increase in ATK, DEF and HP
  • Our allies after cast a non-attack skill will gain 1
    [Rousing Morale]
    stack and Ultimate will gain 2
    [Rousing Morale]
    stacks.
  • At the start of each round, Strange Doctor will enter
    [Mastermind]
    status.
    • If Futurism is in [Control] status, exit that status, gain Futurism 3
      [Dynamo]
      stack and suffer
      [True Form Exposed]
      for 1 round.
  • If all robots are dead, Strange Doctor will suffer
    [True Form Exposed]
    for 3 rounds.
  • Strange Doctor rotation:
    • First, it will buff DMG bonus to all enemies and gain Futurism 1
      [Dynamo]
      stack and +1 Moxie.
    • Second, it will attack 2 units with the highest HP percentages, inflict Ultimate Might -15% for 1 round, if there’s no Test robot 2 on the field, summon it (up to 4 enemies on field).
    • Repeat the above two steps.
    • Ultimate will gain a Shield for 2 rounds, enter prepare status for 2 rounds then dispel all [Neg Status] on self, gain Futurism with 2
      [Dynamo]
      stack, DMG bonus +50% and +5 Moxie to all enemies.
  • Futurism mechanism:
    • Enters battle, gain 4
      [Dynamo]
      stacks.
    • At the end of each turn or being attacked, lose 1
      [Dynamo]
      stack.
    • If lose all
      [Dynamo]
      stacks, enter
      [Staggered]
      status.
    • When in
      [Burn]
      ,
      [Poison]
      and
      [Corrode]
      status, DMG Dealt -25%.
    • Every turn, it attacks 2 units with the highest HP percentages.
  • Test robots 1 and 2 have DMG taken fixed at 1, and has total HP of 8 and 3.

Crisis Column mode

  • If any enemies use Ultimates, gain 2
    [Sturdiness]
    stacks to all enemies.

Strategy and tips

  • Prioritize Beast & Plant units since they deal more damage.
  • Focus on dealing with Futurism and Strange Doctor.
  • When Futurism is not in
    [Staggered]
    status, the Strange Doctor will takes masively reduce Reality DMG, so focus on dealing with Futurism until he is in
    [Staggered]
    , then focus on dealing with the Strange Doctor.
  • Inflict DoT effects like
    [Burn]
    ,
    [Poison]
    and
    [Corrode]
    will reduce the DMG dealt by Futurism and deals DMG to other test robots.

Rousing Morale

Pos Status

DMG Bonus +50% when attacking (reduced by 1 stack on trigger).

Mastermind

Pos Status

Will not be prioritized as a secondary target. When attacked by Reality DMG, DMG Taken Reduction +50% (undispellable).

Dynamo

Special

A product of technology used for meticulous machinery (up to 30 stacks, undispellable).

True Form Exposed

Neg Status

When attacked by Reality DMG, DMG Taken Reduction -100%, and cannot enter
[Mastermind]
status (undispellable).

Staggered

Special

Cannot act.

Burn

Neg Status

Healing Taken -15% (unstackable). At the end of the round, takes (the holder’s ATK x4%) Genesis DMG (stackable up to 30 times, multiple stacks of Burn is regarded as 1 [Neg Status], removes 50% of the stacks when triggered).

Poison

Neg Status

At the end of the round, takes Genesis DMG based on the user’s ATK (stackable).

Corrode

Neg Status

When a round ends, takes Genesis DMG based on the carrier’s current HP.

Sturdiness

Pos Status

When attacked, DMG Taken Reduction +25% (-1 stack after trigger).
