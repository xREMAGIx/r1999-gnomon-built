Darkness of the Abyss

Reality

Stage mechanism

  • Star afflatus units gain 20% stats increase in ATK, DEF and HP
  • The boss takes 75% more Genesis DMG.
  • It self-cleanses all [Neg Status] at end of turn.
  • If it didn’t take damage in one turn, it will gain 2 layers of
    [Sturdiness]
    .
  • Every 2 rounds, the boss will aim at 2 units with the highest HP percentages left and inflict 4 layers of
    [Frostbite]
    .
  • Its ultimate will inflict 5 stacks of
    [Frostbite]
    immediately, cause whole team to be
    [Freeze]
    .
  • The tentacles will attack the unit with the highest HP percentage left at turn end, and give 1 stack of
    [Sturdiness]
    to self and the boss. If at 4 moxie, they will cast Ultimate and inflict
    [Freeze]
    on the target instead.
  • When a tentacle is killed by your attacks, the boss will take Genesis DMG equal to 15% Max HP.
  • When DotA’s Eureka is full, it activates a shield phase, where failing to destroy the shield within 3 rounds leads to the removal of all buffs from your team and a powerful AoE attack followed by
    [Freeze]
    .

Crisis Column mode

  • After casting their Ultimates, DotA and its Tentacles gain a buff, which cannot be dispelled, that reduces incoming damage.
  • 4 stacks of
    [Frostbite]
    will be applied per attack instead of 2.

Strategy and tips

  • Prioritize Genesis DMG & Star units since they deal more damage.
  • Avoid relying on debuff application due to DotA’s end-round cleanse.
  • Attack the boss every turn to prevent it from gaining
    [Sturdiness]
    .
  • [Frostbite]
    is a debuff that can be cleansed and immunity to it.
  • [Frostbite]
    stacks can be removed by taking active actions twice per turn.
  • When DotA in shield phase, focus on breaking its shield with all your attacks instead of trying to survive.

Crisis Column mode

  • Preventing the execution of most ultimates is key by using character with
    [Silence]
    or Moxie reduction skills.

Sturdiness

Pos Status

When attacked, DMG Taken Reduction +25% (-1 stack after trigger).

Frostbite

Neg Status

When there are 5 stacks, inflicts
[Freeze]
status. If already in
[Freeze]
status, status duration +1 round.

Freeze

Control

Cannot act. Dispels the effect after taking Mental DMG attack.

Silence

Control

Cannot cast Buff, Debuff, Heal, or Counter-type incantations.
